Let's Play!

Rules:

Beep Boop will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers that contain a 1 become "beep"
  2. Numbers that contain a 2 become "boop"
  3. Numbers that contain 3 and 5 become "beepboop"
